# 🎯 Workshop Objectives

By the end of this workshop, participants will be able to:

- Understand challenges in the research lifecycle
- Explore modern AI research tools
- Speed up literature review using AI
- Improve academic writing productivity
- Learn ethical use of AI in research

---

# 🧠 Research Challenges

Research can be overwhelming because of:

- 📚 Literature overload
- ⏳ Time-consuming reading
- ✍️ Academic writing difficulties
- 🔍 Identifying research gaps
- 📄 Organizing references

AI tools help automate repetitive tasks and improve productivity.

---

# 💡 Idea Generation Tools

## 🔹 Perplexity AI

🔗 https://www.perplexity.ai/

### Features
- AI-powered search engine
- Research brainstorming
- Topic summaries
- Combines web + academic sources

### Strengths
- Fast idea generation
- Conversational interface

### Limitations
- Can hallucinate facts
- Requires fact-checking

---

## 🔹 Semantic Scholar

🔗 https://www.semanticscholar.org/

### Features
- Academic paper discovery
- Citation analysis
- AI summaries
- Influential paper recommendations

### Strengths
- Excellent academic search
- Semantic-based recommendations

### Limitations
- Limited to academic datasets

---

# 📚 Literature Review Tools

---

## 🔹 Elicit

🔗 https://elicit.com/

### Features
- Automates literature review
- Extracts findings from papers
- Research summaries
- Study comparison

### Best For
- Systematic literature review
- Research synthesis

---

## 🔹 Consensus

🔗 https://consensus.app/

### Features
- Evidence-based answers
- Searches scientific literature
- Summarizes scientific consensus

### Best For
- Fact-checking research claims
- Scientific evidence summaries

---

## 🔹 Research Rabbit

🔗 https://researchrabbitapp.com/home

### Features
- Citation mapping
- Research visualization
- Discover related papers
- Explore author networks

### Best For
- Building literature maps
- Discovering connected research

---

# 📖 Reading & Comprehension Tools

---

## 🔹 SciSpace

🔗 https://scispace.com/

### Features
- Chat with PDFs
- Explain difficult papers
- AI research copilot
- Simplify equations & methods

### Additional Tool
🔗 https://scispace.com/extract-data

### Best For
- Understanding complex research papers
- Extracting research findings

# ✍️ Academic Writing & Editing
